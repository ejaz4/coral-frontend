Coral Guidelines

Coral believes in the freedom of speech, but there are some things we must regulate on our site.


1. No NSFW Content

What is NSFW content?

NSFW (Not Safe for Work) content is content that contains sexual/adult and other explicit material.

Why is NSFW content banned?

We prohibit NSFW because we aim to be a service that anyone can watch. Having content suitable for all ages will help us reach to this goal.

Does this mean no swearing?

While Coral does not currently allow material such as listed above, we do allow swearing, just not slurs (derogatory terms relating to race, country of origin, religion, etc.)


2. No violation of United States laws

Coral is based in the United States, so we along with your help are required to abide by the United States laws that governs us.


3. No violation of copyright laws

We know that others have failed their users when enforcing this law, but it's important for the survival of Coral. We promise to let you exercise your rights of Fair Use, as it is NOT a violation of copyright laws, it is therefore allowed on our service.


What happens if I violate these rules?

Currently, videos are manually screened before they are published, so videos violating our guidelines will not be published or therefore punished. Please make sure your video follows our guidelines before submitting a video to save both your time, as well as the reviewer's.
