About Coral

Founded at the end of 2020, Coral is a new take on the video distribution industry that plans to keep user privacy first.

If at any time you face an issue or have questions about our site, feel free to contact us via support@coral.video


What are our goals?

Our goal is to eventually generate enough revenue from Coral for it to be able to maintain server costs on its own, as well as provide revenue.

We also would like you, the creator, to also be able to profit off of your content. We are still brainstorming ideas such as advertising that also respect user privacy.
